The owners of Soft supreme How we came to a start How we assist the community
Vincent and Jacque started Soft Supreme in 2005. They always wanted to start a business together, but they were not expecting it to be a bed-selling-company. Vincent was planing to start a web development company, when Jacque came to him with a business idea. Vincent loved it so much that Soft Supreme was born in that same conversation. We help the community by using our remainingsupplies and make affordable mattresses, blankets or pillows wich we either sponser or sell without a profit. When were a bigger company with a franchise or two we can contribute more.
